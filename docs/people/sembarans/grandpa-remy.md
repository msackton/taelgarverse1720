# Grandpa Remy
>[!info]+ Biographical Info
> A [Sembaran](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>) [human](<../../species/humans/humans.md>) (he/him)
> 
>> 
>>  Met with the [Heroes of Cleenseau](<../pcs/cleenseau/heroes-of-cleenseau.md>) on October 21st, 1719 in [Taviose](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/cleenseau-region/taviose.md>), [Cleenseau](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/cleenseau-region/cleenseau/cleenseau.md>), the [Barony of Aveil](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/barony-of-aveil.md>) 

Grandpa Remy is well-liked for his off-color jokes and his fondness for apple brandy. He was saved from death by [Izgil's](<../pcs/cleenseau/izgil-moonseeker.md>) magic and has become one of the biggest boosters of the [Heroes of Cleenseau](<../pcs/cleenseau/heroes-of-cleenseau.md>).
